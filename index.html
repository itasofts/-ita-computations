About ita computations

ita computations is a powerful geospatial app designed to simplify complex calculations. Whether you're computing bearings, distances, areas, or generating detailed sheets, ita computations has you covered. Available for Android, iOS, and Windows, it’s the perfect tool for professionals and enthusiasts alike.

  • Perform geospatial calculations offline.
  • Generate Bearing and Distance sheets, Area Computation sheets, Plan Data sheets, and Beacon Index sheets.
  • Supports light and dark modes for enhanced usability.
