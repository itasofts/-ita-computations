About ITA-Computations

ITA-Computations is a powerful geospatial app designed to simplify complex calculations. Whether you're computing bearings, distances, areas, or generating detailed sheets, ITA-Computations has you covered.

  • Perform geospatial calculations offline.
  • Generate Bearing and Distance sheets, Area Computation sheets, Plan Data sheets, and Beacon Index sheets.
  • Supports light and dark modes for enhanced usability.

Features

Bearing and Distance Calculation

Compute bearings and distances between coordinates with ease.

Area Calculation

Calculate areas from coordinates and generate detailed computation sheets.
